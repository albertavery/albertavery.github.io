Hi!

I'm Albert Avery, currently a student majoring in Business Administration – Computer Information Systems at CSU Fresno State. I enjoy working with computers, and I've been messing around with them at a young age. I graduated from Clovis High School in 2016, where I participated in multiple ensembles, such as their marching band, string orchestra, wind ensemble and indoor percussion. During my time there, our percussion ensemble won the SJVCGPR Championship title – in the "World Class" division from 2013 to 2016. We also traveled to Dayton, Ohio, in 2015 and 2016 to participate in the WGI World Championships.

Currently, I am a front ensemble technician and pit writer at Clovis High. I work with the students in their front ensemble, orchestrate the mallet instruments and arrange their electronics. I am also a member of the Red Wave Indoor Percussion Ensemble for their inaugural year. In addition, I am a sound engineer for the Fresno State Bulldog Marching Band, which is the largest student organization at Fresno State, with as many as 250 members. I spend my free time either playing video games, or writing music. I enjoy hanging out with people, and learning about new things! Feel free to check out my other pages on this website, where you can find my other projects.
