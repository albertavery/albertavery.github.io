Hi!

I'm Albert Avery, currently a student majoring in Business Administration - Computer Information Systems at CSU Fresno State. I enjoy working with computers, and I've been messing around with them at a young age. I graduated from Clovis High School in 2016, where I participated in multiple ensembles, such as their marching band, string orchestra, wind ensemble and indoor percussion. During my time there, our percussion ensemble won the SJVCGPR Championship title - in the "World Class" division from 2013 to 2016. We also traveled to Dayton, Ohio, in 2015 and 2016 to participate in the WGI World Championships. Currently, I am teaching at Clovis High, where I work with their front ensemble and arrange their electronics. I am also a member of the Fresno State Indoor Percussion Ensemble for their inaugural year. I spend my free time either playing video games, or writing music. I enjoy hanging out with people, and learning about new things! Feel free to check out my other pages on this website, where you can find my other projects.
